About Institute

Empowering Future Engineers

Murarai Government Polytechnic is a leading technical institution under the Government of West Bengal dedicated to quality technical education.

The institute currently offers Diploma Programs in:

  • Computer Science & Technology (CST)
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Survey Engineering (SE)

Our mission is to build skilled professionals ready for industry and higher education.
